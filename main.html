Glasgow 2021

COP26
UNITED NATIONS
CLIMATE CONFERENCE

For the first time, the UK will host the 26th UN Climate Change Conference of the Parties (COP26) at the Scottish Event Campus (SEC) in Glasgow on 1 – 12 November 2021.

1st NOVEMBER ~ 12th NOVEMBER 2021

@ The Scottish Event Campus (SEC),
Glasgow, U.K.

Main Program

Lectures

Speakers from all nations of the world will deliver addresses on the actions required to effectively combat climate change.

Forums

Open forums will be held to collectively discuss proposed solutions and inform policymakers around the world.

Plenaries

Observe the plenaries held by delegates of UN member states as they consider the motions put forward to tackle climate change.

Workshop

Workshops on a number of topics will take place, to share all kinds of knowledge and skills that can be used in the fight against climate change.

Social

Evening social events will take place at various venues in Glasgow to allow attendees to meet others who share their vision for the Earth's future.

Featured Speakers

Eyüp Davidse

YouTube Influencer

Eyüp Davidse is a celebrated social media advocate for the environment, using both YouTube and, more recently, Loom as platforms to connect with and unite his millions of admirers in the common cause of climate justice.

Wilson Defoe

Former CEO of TacMedCent

Wilson Defoe, initially a prodigous computer hacker, is best-known for his wildly successful online medical supply store. But since the acquisition of TacMedCent by Amazon in July 2020, Mr. Defoe has turned his attention to the greatest wound of all: climate change.

Carolina Roy

Senior Director, Head of Group Public Affairs and Sustainability Danfoss

Carolina Roy leads Danfoss’ global public affairs team, and is responsible for government relations, thought leadership and sustainability strategy to accelerate technology development and implementation in the areas of e.g. urban efficiency and sustainable food supply.

Oyeleke Piccard

Vice President Sustainability, Polymetal

As Vice President Sustainability and Climate, Oyeleke Piccard has been responsible for Polymetal's sustainability strategy and agenda since 2018. Having started his career at BP in 2005, Oyeleke has 14 years of experience in the energy sector.

Roberta Pocero

Strategic Marketing Group Director, Picadeli

Roberta Pocero is Strategic Marketing Group Director of Nordic healthy food and convenience company Picadeli Group. An experienced international executive, Pocero has also worked as Chief Executive of numerous international retail players.

Josef von Bürke

Vice President, British Petroleum

Josef works with clients to leverage digital technology and accelerate their energy transition projects, especially in the area of smart building, e-mobility and smart energy.
